## 1. G3 (yosemite) — FULLY VALIDATED on hardware ✅

- **HUD garbage was 3D models**, not textures: the status bar renders 3 MD3
  models into HUD viewports (`cg_draw.c CG_DrawStatusBar`); the Rage 128 draws
  them as garbage. Fix: `cg_draw3dIcons 0` (2D-icon fallback) + `cg_lagometer 0`
  (the red/green/blue net-graph box). Verified by screenshot.
- **Textures**: `r_picmip 3` (1/8 res) was the "very basic" look. Clean A/B @
  1024×768: picmip 3=28.2, 1=27.0, 0=20.8 fps (picmip 0 thrashes the 16 MB VRAM —
  the Rage 128 has **no S3TC**). picmip 1 is the sweet spot.
- **Effects restored**: the survival `r_vertexlight 1` had collapsed every map
  shader to one flat stage (no lit/animated walls, no portals/glows). Now
  `r_vertexlight 0` (lightmaps + full shaders) + `r_dynamiclight 1` + `r_flares 1`
  at **800×600** = **22.1 fps** (clears the 20 floor). Screenshots confirm proper
  lighting + rocket glow. Per your "effects > fps" preference.
- **Sound was the #1 CPU cost** (profiled, ~29% of frame). The SDL backend rate
  is `s_sdlSpeed` (NOT `s_khz`); `s_sdlSpeed 11025` halves the scalar mix work:
  640×480 went 47.7→54.6 fps (sound-off ceiling 61.0). See `docs/PROFILING.md`.

## 2. Machine auto-config — VALIDATED ✅

`code/qcommon/common.c`: at startup the engine reads `hw.model`, applies a
compile-time per-arch baseline (`autoexec-ppc750/ppc7400/x86_64`) then a
per-machine overlay, both from the `.app` bundle via CFBundle. One universal
`.app` self-tunes on every Mac (mirrors the QuakeSpasm/Quake2 ports). `+set
com_archAutoexec 0` opts out (bench scripts use this). Validated on yosemite:
with no `baseq3/autoexec.cfg`, the `.app` self-applied 1024-era settings purely
from `PowerMac1,1`. `make-app.sh` bundles all 10 cfgs into Resources/.
